Fitness Classes

Yoga

Yoga is a great way to improve flexibility, reduce stress, and promote relaxation. Our classes are suitable for all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

Zumba

Zumba is a fun and energetic dance workout that combines Latin and international music with easy-to-follow dance moves. Our classes are designed to help you burn calories and improve cardiovascular fitness.

Hardcore Training

Our hardcore training classes are designed to help you build strength, increase muscle mass, and improve your overall fitness level. Our experienced trainers will push you to your limits and help you achieve your fitness goals.

Weight Lifting

Weight lifting is a great way to build strength, increase muscle mass, and improve your overall fitness level. Our classes are suitable for all levels, from beginners to advanced lifters, and our experienced trainers will guide you through proper form and technique.

Cardio

Our cardio classes are designed to help you improve cardiovascular fitness, burn calories, and lose weight. We offer a variety of classes, including cycling, kickboxing, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT).

Functional

Functional fitness training is a type of strength training that readies your body for daily activities. These exercises equip you for the most important type of physical fitness, the kind that preps you for real-life, daily living stuff like bending, twisting, lifting, loading, pushing, pulling, squatting and hauling.
